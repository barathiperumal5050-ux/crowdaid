HELPER SAFETY BRIEF

Read before entering the live room
  • 🛑
    Only help if YOU are safe. Do not enter traffic zones, fire areas, or structurally unsafe buildings. Your life comes first.
  • 📍
    Your location is shared only inside this room. It is permanently deleted the moment the room closes. No data is retained.
  • 📵
    If you stop moving for 2+ minutes while in the room, CrowdAid will check if you're okay and notify your emergency contacts.
  • 📲
    No internet? CrowdAid auto-switches to SMS fallback mode to continue coordinating nearby helpers.
  • 🏥
    Follow task assignments in the room. Do only what's assigned to you. Do not attempt tasks beyond your skill level.
⚖️ Good Samaritan Law Protection: In most countries, individuals who provide emergency assistance in good faith are legally protected from civil and criminal liability. You are covered when acting reasonably and within your capabilities. CrowdAid displays jurisdiction-specific information inside the room.
📡 Offline SMS Fallback: If your internet connection drops, this platform automatically sends SMS alerts to nearby registered volunteers and your emergency contacts — so coordination never stops.

By entering, you confirm you've read and understood the safety guidelines.
